Fever & Pain Medicines

Paracetamol

Usage: Fever and mild to moderate pain

Dosage: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours

Precautions: Do not exceed 4000mg in 24 hours

Ibuprofen

Usage: Pain and inflammation

Dosage: 200-400mg every 4-6 hours

Precautions: Take with food

Cold & Cough Medicines

Dextromethorphan

Usage: Dry cough suppression

Dosage: 10-20mg every 4 hours

Precautions: Avoid alcohol, may cause drowsiness

Ambroxol

Usage: Productive cough

Dosage: 30mg three times daily

Precautions: Take with meals

Digestive Health Medicines

Omeprazole

Usage: Acid reflux and indigestion

Dosage: 20mg once daily

Precautions: Take before meals

Metronidazole

Usage: Stomach infections

Dosage: 400mg three times daily

Precautions: Complete full course as prescribed

Allergy Medicines

Cetirizine

Usage: Allergies and hay fever

Dosage: 10mg once daily

Precautions: May cause slight drowsiness
